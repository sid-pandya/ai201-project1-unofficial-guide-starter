Better to BART or to Drive
Traffic, Trains & Transit
I just moved to San Jose not too long ago and I am enrolled at CSUEB. I live a few minutes from Berryessa station. I drove to campus almost everyday last semester and it costed me about 75$ every four days to fill up on gas. Now the convenience there is that I just take myself. I can leave pretty much whenever and get to class on time. Now with rising costs of everything, I am wanting to take the BART but the time allocation is kind of ridiculous. Once I get done with the BART, AC60 takes an hour to get to campus from the BART station when it's only a few minute drive. So I am wondering, are there others who go from SJ to Hayward using public transit and what is your schedule like? How early do you leave for class and when are you able to be back home.
