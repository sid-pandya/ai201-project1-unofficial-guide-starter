Pretty much any good Thai place will be veggie friendly. We like Sisaket in Martinez, Thai Home Cafe in pleasant hill, and Thai Taste in San Ramon.

Field is also fairly open for Italian- we like Locanda Positano. Benvenuti is also good but fewer veggie options right on the menu.

Also, there’s a Chinese place in Martinez that has a whole pretty tasty fake chicken section! Bamboo Garden.
